About Us

About us

who we are ?

Elegance Roofing and restoration LLC is Founded by a professional and licensed insurance adjuster  who has wealth of experience in insurance Claims. We are privately owned and operated serving Atlanta Ga and beyond. We take pride in our exceptional roof installations, roofing repairs and Home renovations.

Our mission

Our focus is to keep our  clients building leak free and water tight. Help our clients get their roof covered by insurance and avoid out of pocket expenses. We are experts at handling the coverages and claims. We don’t just put a excellent roof over your head ,but bring a “touch of elegance to your home”.

Core Value

Our company  is a business with strong core values built on  trust and care for  our customers and our work. 90% of our business comes from customer referrals and reviews, which is a testament to the outstanding work we put into every project. Our dedication and professionalism  drives us to new levels of success and excellence in roofing. By setting the highest standards in workmanship, materials and safety, we are able to deliver the best solutions for all of our customer’s needs. We always tell our customers “Give us 10% of your trust and we will earn 90%.

Services

Elegance Roofing and restoration is here to provide you with professional roof replacement and repair services. As licensed insurance Claim adjusters we work closely  with your insurance provider following a hail and wind storm or other incident to repair your home’s exterior to just as good, if not better, condition.

We are determined to earn the trust and confidence of our customers. We pride ourselves on our amazing customer service, quality workmanship, and keen attention to detail.  Expect satisfying results if you choose us as your contractor. Also, our company is fully licensed, bonded, and insured for your protection.
